1. I forgot my password. How can I get a new one?
Use the Reset Password function.
2. How long will a bid remain active?
Each bid runs for a maximum of 48 hours and the lowest bid wins the order.
3. How do I update my inventory and view my past bids?
You can update your inventory by selecting or deselecting items on the catalogue page and view your past bids on the bidding history page.
4. Can you let me know when the bid ends?
We will let you know when the bid expires and we will notify you regardless if you win or lose the bid. You may check your bid status from your account page. We will also email you regarding your bid status.
